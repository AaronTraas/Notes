# Goan Pork or Lamb Vindaloo 

## Ingredients:
1 (3–3.5 lb) boneless pork butt roast, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 large onion, chopped fine
1/3 cup coconut vinegar 

_spice paste:_
3/4 cup water
1 (2-inch) piece ginger, peeled and sliced
6 garlic cloves
3 tablespoons Kashmiri chile powder 
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon ground cumin
2 teaspoons table salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/4–1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

## Directions:
1. Make the spice paste: Blend water, ginger, garlic, chile powder, paprika, cumin, salt, pepper, cayenne (if using), cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and nutmeg until smooth.
2. Marinate pork: Mix the spice paste with the pork and let it sit for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
Sauté onions: Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Cook chopped onion until soft and golden (7–9 minutes).
3. Cook in oven: Add the pork mixture, spread it evenly, and cook for 40 minutes covered. Stir in vinegar, cover, and cook for 20 minutes. Uncover and continue cooking until pork is fork-tender (20–30 minutes).
4. Rest and serve: Let stand uncovered for 10 minutes before serving.
