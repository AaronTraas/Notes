# Beef Noodle Casserole

From [Brian Lagerstrom](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RwO10-czkLg)

## Intredients

- 12 oz large elbow macaroni
- 1½ lb ground beef
- medium onion, diced
- 8-10 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 lb crimini mushrooms, sliced
- 25g tomato paste
- 50g flour
- 400ml beef stock
- 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 25g Better Than Boullion, beef flavor stock concentrate
- 30g dijon mustard
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 75g sour cream
- 1/2 lb whole-milk mozzarella, shredded
- 50g grated parmesan cheese
- fried onions
- grated parm

## Directions

1. Cook pasta in boiling water until al dente. Drain, set aside.
2. In a dutch ovem brown ground beef over medium heat.
3. When beef is no longer pink, add onion, garlic, and a heavy pinch of salt. Cook until onions are soft.
4. Add mushrooms, cook over medium heat. Add a splash of water, stock, white wine, or spirit to deglaze.
5. Once mushrooms start to brown, add tomato paste and flour. Stir and cook the raw taste off both.
6. 1. Add Worcestershire sauce, stock concentrate, dijon mustard, and heavy cream and stir to combine.
7. Fold in pasta with cheese to combine.
8. Top with fried onions and optionally more parmesan.
9. Bake at 425F for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

## Sausage, Veggies, and Spätzle

### Ingredients:

- Fresh sausage (Italian, Kielbasa, Bratwurst, etc.)
- Onions, sliced
- Other veggies - peppers, peas, spinach, chard, etc.
- Salt
- Olive oil
- Optional: beer or wine
- [Spätzle](./Spaetzle.html)

### Directions

1. Add oil to pan, set burner to medium-low. Add onions to pan, sprinkle liberally with salt, and cook slowly until sweet and jammy.
2. Add other veggies, cook to desired texture.
3. Brown sausage under broiler or on grill
4. Add sausages to pan.
5. Optional: deglaze with beer/wine. Reduce into a syrupy sauce. 
6. Serve over [Spätzle](./Spaetzle.html)
