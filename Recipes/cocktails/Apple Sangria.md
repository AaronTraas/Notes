# Apple "Sangria"

## Ingredients

- 2 oz Apple Cider
- 1 oz White wine
- 1/2 oz Caramel Vodka

### Garnish
- 1 tsp granulated sugar
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Combine cinnamon and sugar
2. Wet glass and rim with cinnamon sugar 
3. Combine cidar, wine, and vodka. Stir.
4. Chill for 1-2 hours or serve in glass over ice.
