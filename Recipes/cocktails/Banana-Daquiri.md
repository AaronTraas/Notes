# Banana Daquiri

- 2oz aged white rum
- 3/4 oz creme de banane (preferred, Tempus Fugit)
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz cinnamon demerara syrup
- garnish: lime wheel, cinnamon stick
