# Lady Laura

I had this at a [Kokuyo Coctail Bar](https://www.kokuyobar.com/), in Roselle, NJ. Really lovely, low-ABV cocktail. Tart, slightly sweet, great flavor, and way too easy to drink fast. They used Bounty coconut rum, which is 25%, and steeped it with hibiscus. I'm going to try a version with Malibu, because I have a bottle I want to get rid of, and it's similar in strength.

It had "house-made orgeat", but I couldn't taste it. Not sure how necessary it is.

The amounts are my guesses, as are the directions, though I'm much more certain about those

## Ingredients

- 2oz Hibiscus-infused coconut rum cordial (Malibu, Bounty)
- 1oz pineapple juice
- 1/2 oz lemon juice
- 1oz orgeat

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to shaker with ice, and shake
2. Strain into hurricane glass or similar filled with crushed ice or pebble ice.
3. Garnish with... purple flower? Pineapple wedge?
