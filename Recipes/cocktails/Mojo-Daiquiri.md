# Mojo Daiquiri

My attempt at building a non-carbonated daiquiri with mint-washed rum. A friend, who really likes mojitos, has developed a sensitivity to carbonated beverages. 

## Ingredients:

- 2½ oz mint-washed rum cordial
- 1 oz fresh lime juice 
- ½ oz filtered water 

## Directions:

1. Add white cordial, lime, and water to cocktail shaker. Shake over ice.
2. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora or wine glass.

# Mint-Washed Rum Cordial

## Ingredients:

- 20 grams mint leaves
- 100 grams sugar
- 400 mL white rum 

## Directions:

- Wash and dry mint. 
- Toss sugar and mint together, and add to vacuum bag. Vacuum seal.
- Refrigerate for 24 hours. 
- Cut open bag, add white rum, and re-seal bag. Ensure sugar is dissolved.
- Freeze for 24 hours. 
- Filter infused rum through fine-mesh strainer into a bottle.
- Store refrigerated for up to a month.
