# Clover Club

A pink drink a man could love. 

You know what a real man drinks? Whatever the fuck he wants.

## Ingredients:

***Option 1: with vermouth***
- 1 ½ oz gin
- ½ oz dry vermouth
- ½ oz lemon juice
- ½ oz raspberry syrup 
- ½ oz egg white (about ½ an egg white)  

***Option 2: no vermouth***
- 2 oz gin
- ½ oz lemon juice
- ½ oz raspberry syrup 
- ½ oz egg white (about ½ an egg white)  

​## Hardware:

- Cocktail shaker
- Strainer
- Coupe or Nick & Nora glass

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker,  Dry shake for about 6 seconds.  
2. Then add ice and shake vigorously for 8-10 seconds.  
3. Strain into a chilled glass. 
4. Garnish with 3 fresh raspberries in a skewer.

# Raspberry Syrup

## Ingredients:

- 1 cups sugar
- ½ cup water
- ½ cup raspberries

## Directions:

1. Crush raspberries with a muddler.
2. Add the sugar and water to small sauce pot, heat over low heat, and stir until sugar is mostly dissolved. Do not bring to a boil.
3. Remove from heat, stir in raspberries. 
4. Let rest for 30 minutes, then stir mixture. 
5. Strain syrup through a fine mesh strainer to remove pulp and seeds.
6. Refrigerate; will keep for about 1 month. Optionally, add 1 oz vodka to stabilize
