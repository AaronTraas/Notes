# Royal Hawaiian

Classic tiki drink from the Royal Hawaiian hotel. Very pretty if made with Empress 1908 Gin.

## Ingredients:

- 1½ oz Empress 1908 Indigo Gin or a London dry gin ([See Note](#notes))
- 1 oz Pimeapple juice
- ½ oz Fresh squeezed lemon juice
- ½ oz orgeat syrup 

## Directions:

- Add pineapple juice, lemon juice, and orgeat to shaker.
- Shake vigorously over ice.
- Strain into tall glass with crushed or pebble ice.
- Gently float gin on top.
- Garnish with pineapple wedge, pineapple fronds, and/or flowers

## Notes:

- Original recipe from [Empress Gin](https://empressgin.com/cocktail/royal-hawaiian/)
- The Empress 1908 is a beautiful purple, and makes this drink pretty. But a london dry gin is good too.
