# Dirty Daiquiri

A daiquiri for those of us that don't particularly like daiquiris. Hemingway would hate this. I love it. The addition of the splash of [Mamajuana](./Mamajuana.html) completely changes the drink. 

## Ingredients:

- 2 oz Plantaray 3-Star white rum
- ¼ oz [Mamajuana](./Mamajuana.html)
- 1 oz fresh lime juice 
- 1 oz simple syrup 

## Directions:

1. Add white rum, lime, and simple syrup to cocktail shaker. Shake over ice.
2. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora or wine glass. 
3. Drizzle mamajuana on top. Serve.
