# Meatloaf

Work in progress. Trying to replace the salt with beef stock concentrate, which has a fair bit of salt, but also a lot of other things as well.
## Ingredients

- 2 lb 80% lean ground beef
- 1 cup crushed stale bread or breadcrumbs
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 1 medium onion, minced
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tbsp milk
- 2 teaspoons Better than Bullion beef base (or 1 tsp salt)
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. Saute onions until soft and tender.
2. Add all ingredients to a large mixing bowl. Mix by hand until mixture is smooth. 
3. Cover and refrigerate mixture for at least 30 minutes.
4. Preheat oven to 375°F.
5. Form into an oblong loaf. Place on parchment-lined sheet pan.
6. Bake at 375°F until internal temperature of 160°F
7. Optional: baste with glaze (ketchup, BBQ sauce, etc.) and broil until caramelized.
8. Let meatloaf rest for 10-15 minutes at room temperature.
9. Slice and serve.
