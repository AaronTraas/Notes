# Sourdough Buttermilk Waffles 

Ingredients:

- 8oz/227g sourdough starter (active or discard)
- 4oz/113g buttermilk 
- 3oz/85g oil 
- 1 large egg
- 1oz/28g/2 Tbsp sugar
- 1 Tbsp vanilla extract 
- 1.8oz/51g starch (corn, tapioca, potato, arrowroot, etc.)
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp kosher salt
