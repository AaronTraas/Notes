# Biscuits

Developing. Based on [Tyler Florence's recipe](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/tyler-florence/better-buttermilk-biscuits-recipe-1908148)

## Ingredients

 - 2 cups all-purpose flour
 - ½ cup vegetable shortening, chilled, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
 - ¾ cup buttermilk 
 - 1 tablespoon sugar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 teaspoons baking powder
 - ½ teaspoon baking soda

## Directions

1. Pre-heat oven to 400°F.
2. In a large mixing bowl, add dry ingredients and whisk together to combine. 
3. Cut in  shortening with a pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. 
4. Make a well in the center and add buttermilk. 
5. Mix dry ingredients into buttermilk with your hands until it forms a sticky dough.
6. Turn dough out onto floured surface. Fold the dough over itself 3–4 times. 
7. Roll dough out ¾-inch thick. 
8. Cut with a 3-inch biscuit cutter. Gather scraps and repeat.
9. Transfer dough rounds to a sheet pan lined with parchment.  
10. Make a dimple in the center of each biscuit. 
11. Brush with melted butter. 
12. Bake for 15 minutes until golden brown.
